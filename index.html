First, a lesson on soap...

So why us?

Falling Water was established in 1999 by a traveling salesman named Tyler Durdan. As a child, Tyler had always loved the way soap smelt and how it seemed to always clean better than body wash, but was never impressed with the dry feeling most soaps left after use. So he decided to stop traveling door to door selling stereos, and become a full time soap-maker. After many years of trial and error (and a few explosions), Tyler had finally found the perfect combination to make the perfect soap. And just like that Falling Water was born- with 22 years of experience behind our belt, we're sure you'll find the perfect soap for you.
